How Grad-CAM Works

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping (Grad-CAM) highlights the regions in the image that the model considers most important for its prediction. Warmer colors (red/yellow) indicate areas of high importance, while cooler colors (blue/green) show less relevant regions.

🔴 Red Zones

High importance - Model focused heavily on these areas

🟡 Yellow Zones

Medium importance - Supporting evidence for decision

🔵 Blue Zones

Low importance - Background or less relevant areas

About This System

🎯 Purpose

This system uses deep learning to analyze histopathology images and provide explainable predictions for breast cancer diagnosis. It's designed to assist medical professionals in their diagnostic process.

🧠 Technology

Built with convolutional neural networks (CNN) and Grad-CAM visualization for interpretable AI. The model has been trained on histopathology images to distinguish between benign and malignant tissue.

📋 Features

  • AI-powered image classification
  • Grad-CAM explainability heatmaps
  • Confidence scoring
  • Treatment category recommendations
  • Breast cancer information chatbot
